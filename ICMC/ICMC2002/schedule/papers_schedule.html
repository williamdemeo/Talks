ICMC2002 Paper Schedule
September 17-21 2002

Papers and demonstrations are scheduled in three different rooms in the School of Music building, Artisten: the Chamber Music Hall (A302), the Organ Hall (B301), and the Lecture Hall (A505). A fourth room in Artisten, class room C310, is available, and may be used for talks that need to be rescheduled or repeated, or spontaneous sessions.
